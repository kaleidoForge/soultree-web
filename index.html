About SoulTree Journal

SoulTree Journal was created with care to give you a simple and safe space to reflect, practice gratitude, and record your thoughts each day.

Inspired by the wisdom of the Sefirot, ten aspects of consciousness, each color and theme invites you to explore a different dimension of your inner world.

The Colors of Consciousness

Every emotion, every reflection, has a color. SoulTree Journal uses the ten aspects of the Sefirot to guide your daily writing journey, bringing clarity to your deepest thoughts.

Keter — The Crown

Connection with the Divine, infinite potential, and purity of intention. Use it for supreme clarity.

Chokmah — Wisdom

Pure intuition, the spark of an idea, wisdom before it takes form. Use it to receive inspiration.

Binah — Understanding

Deep understanding and discernment. Give concrete form to your ideas and comprehend complex patterns.

Chesed — Mercy

Unconditional love, expansion, generosity, abundance. Use it to attract abundance and expand opportunities.

Gevurah — Strength

Healthy boundaries, personal power, and determination. Transform situations that no longer serve you.

Tiferet — Beauty

Balance, harmony, truth, compassion, integration. Create balance in your life and manifest beauty.

Netzach — Victory

Emotion, passion, victory, nature, creativity. Use it for creative projects and cultivating authentic relationships.

Hod — Splendor

Communication, intellect, gratitude, humility. Improve your communication and solve problems logically.

Yesod — Foundation

Subconscious, dreams, fertility, connection. Access your deep intuition and strengthen your spiritual foundation.

Malkuth — Kingdom

Physical manifestation, presence, tangible reality. Bring your visions into the physical world and feel more present.
